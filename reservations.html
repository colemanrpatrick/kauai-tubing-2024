This is a fun tubing adventure unique in all of Hawaii. Equipped with an inner tube and a head lamp, you’ll float down a historic irrigation ditch and through several amazing tunnels and flumes engineered and hand dug around 1870. Along the way, enjoy great views of Kauai’s lush rainforest, mountains and valleys. After your Kauai tubing adventure, your tour guide will lead you to a beautiful picnic area nearby for a delicious lunch. Then enjoy a refreshing swim in a natural swimming hole.

As you float down the tropical waterway, you'll be able to see an amazing engineering feat up close. This historic irrigation ditch and the several amazing tunnels and flumes were hand dug around 1870! Enjoy the wonder and magic of each tunnel, lit by headlamps. The open canals run through some of Kauai's most beautiful and remote land and feature fantastic views of the mountains and Waialeale Crater.

At the end of your tubing adventure, your tour guide will lead you to a beautiful picnic spot nearby for a delicious lunch and a refreshing dip in a natural swimming pool.

special instructions

  • Where and When:

    Check in 30 minutes prior to your requested tour time at the Kauai Backcountry Adventures office, located at 3-4131 Kuhio Highway, Lihue, HI 96766. View Map Look for the wooden fence and a sign that says "Kauai Backcountry Adventures."

  • Restrictions:

    Participants must be 5 years and older AND 43" (110 cm) or taller. Weight limit is 300 pounds (136 kg) Children 15 years and under must be accompanied by an adult 18 years and older. Those 16-17 years old may attend the tour without an adult, however, a parent must come in to check in the minor and sign a waiver. Not recommended for anyone claustrophobic or those with back, hip or knee problems. Women up to the second trimester of pregnancy may participate with written physician approval.

  • Special Notes:

    You can rent a wetsuit for $10 or buy one for $30. Unless extreme weather prohibits, tours go out rain or shine.
